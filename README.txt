Doulos SIL ALU
==============

This font is targetted towards the needs of the Akha and Lahu languages.
The font consists of a Doulos style codepage 1252 font with added
Unicode characters for Akha and Lahu tone marks.

The font family is released under the SIL Open Font License.
	
See the OFL and OFL-FAQ for details of the SIL Open Font License.
See the FONTLOG for information on this and previous releases.
